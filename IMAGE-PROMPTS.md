# Campaign Chaos Image Prompt Pack

Use these prompts to generate local raster assets for `public/img/`. The game is offline-safe and will fall back to the existing SVG art whenever a file is absent.

## 1. Global Style Preamble

Paste this before any single entry below:

```text
Create fictional, tasteful satirical art for a mobile-first Maltese election game called Campaign Chaos. No real public figures, no real party logos, no defamatory likenesses. The mood is sharp political theatre: limestone streets, harbour light, campaign posters, TV-news tension, EU flags, village festa energy, construction cranes, press scrums, ballot boxes, and Maltese civic architecture. Keep the image cohesive across the set with warm limestone highlights, deep broadcast shadows, red and blue campaign accents, and subtle gold details. Make it cinematic but readable on a small phone screen. Avoid text, captions, watermarks, real names, and visible logos.

Style variant A, photoreal editorial photo: realistic camera image, natural faces, credible Maltese locations, editorial news-magazine lighting, 35mm documentary feel, shallow depth of field where appropriate, high detail, grounded satire, no cartoon exaggeration.

Style variant B, vector poster: clean vector-poster illustration, bold shapes, screen-print texture, limited palette of limestone cream, deep brown-black, campaign red, campaign blue, muted gold, crisp silhouettes, high contrast, no gradients that obscure the subject.
```
